Fallen is an alternative rock band, based in Sofia, Bulgaria.

The members are:
Svilen Ninov (Left) - Bass Guitar
Lachezar Yordanov (Center) - Vocal and Guitar
Angel Yankov (Right) - Guitar

The music we play mainly consists of 90s grunge and alternative rock hits, as well as some less known songs from that time.

We also work on some author's songs. So far we have one released and are currently working on a few more.
